Principles

One foundation of learning starts with the ability to listen well. We lead by example, teaching children to imitate actions and sounds. Slowly, we progress to the concept of following instructions.
Basic sports movements and skills introduced in a developmental age appropriate way. For soccer we teach them how to use their feet instead of their hands, For basketball and volleyball we focus on how to toss and bounce the ball.
Our programs uses fun and engaging practices that are age appropriate and that help children build a long lasting appreciation for playing sports over time.
Basic roles and functions of the proper gameplay, concepts of defending and attacking are introduced through mini games. This allows the child to progressively ease in to team play scenarios.
Competition through Team games of 2v2 and 3v3 are introduced, highlighting the values of sportsmanship and teamwork to go with a higher level of physical ability.
